# Weedoshi Diaries (WD) - Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Weedoshi Diaries (WD) is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors, users, and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political beliefs, profession, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior Examples
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting people's privacy and boundaries
- Acknowledging and crediting contributions
- Asking questions when you don't understand something

### Unacceptable Behavior Examples
- Harassment of any kind (sexual, racial, religious, etc.)
- Hate speech or discriminatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Threats of violence or actual violence
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces (GitHub, Nostr, Discord, Twitter, etc.)
- Official project communications
- Public interactions representing the project
- Private interactions related to project business

## Enforcement

### Reporting
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers:
- **Email**: [Contact method - to be added]
- **GitHub**: Create a private security advisory
- **Nostr**: Direct message via #weedoshi community

Include:
- What happened
- Who was involved
- When it happened
- Any relevant links or screenshots
- Why you believe this violates the Code of Conduct

### Confidentiality
We will keep reports confidential and investigate them promptly.

### Consequences
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
1. **Warning**: A private or public warning
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community spaces
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community spaces

The severity of consequences depends on the nature and severity of the violation.

## Guidelines for Cannabis Community

Since Weedoshi Diaries (WD) is cannabis-focused, we add these guidelines:

### Respect Local Laws
- We respect that cannabis laws vary by location
- Members must comply with local laws where they live
- We don't facilitate illegal activity
- We acknowledge cannabis prohibition in many places

### Respect Different Perspectives
- Some people may have medical reasons for using cannabis
- Some people may use cannabis for recreation
- Some people may be studying cannabis agriculture
- We respect all legal, peaceful uses

### No Glorification of Harmful Use
- We don't promote use by minors
- We don't promote impaired driving
- We don't promote unsafe consumption
- We promote responsible, legal use

### Privacy Matters
- Never doxx or expose anyone's growing operation
- Respect people's privacy on what they grow
- Don't share location information
- Protect identities in the decentralized space
